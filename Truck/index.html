ME 1670 Truck Design

About

In my ME 1670 class, we were given a project to design and give a presentation on a product of our choice. I led my team in the modeling of The Traffic Transformer, a truck capable of extending high above other cars to simply drive over traffic This was the final project of the class, the culmination of all the SolidWorks skills we had learned. I designed the roof, parts for the wheel assembly, and the car seats. I also edited and directed edits of models from the team to ensure everything fit together. The project also required a presentation, with an optional animation. I decided to take advantage of this opportunity and learn how to render and animate parts and assemblies in SolidWorks, the final video of which is below.(High Audio Warning!)

Overall, my team and I were very pleased with the result of our work and it seemed the professor also liked it because we got an A on the project.
